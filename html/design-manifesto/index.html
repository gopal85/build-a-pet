Want to design? Consider these principles.


chat_bubble_outline

Limit description.

Less is more. Capture your ideas in minimal amounts of text, and you'll engage your audience.

aspect_ratio

Space relaxes eyes.

Take broad ideas, and put them in large containers. Your visitors will recognize their importance.

wallpaper

Exude unity.

Thematic consistency helps visitors recognize your aesthetic. This can be accomplished with just a few fonts, colors, and patterns.
